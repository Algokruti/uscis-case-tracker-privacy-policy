Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

USCIS Case Tracker ("the App") is developed by Algokruti. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

The App collects only the information you voluntarily enter:

  • USCIS Receipt Numbers — used to check your case status via the official USCIS API
  • Nicknames — optional labels you assign to your cases for easy identification

How Your Data Is Stored

  • All data is stored locally on your device in memory only
  • Data is not persisted — when you close the app, all entered data is cleared
  • We do not store your data on any external servers or databases
  • We do not have access to any data you enter in the App

How Your Data Is Used

Receipt numbers you enter are sent directly to the official USCIS API (uscis.gov) to retrieve your case status. This communication happens directly between your device and the USCIS servers. We do not act as an intermediary and do not intercept, store, or log these requests.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or analytics
  • No usage tracking or behavioral data
  • No advertising data
  • No cookies or similar tracking technologies

Third-Party Services

The App communicates only with the official USCIS API (uscis.gov) to retrieve case status information. No other third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks are used.

Account Registration

The App does not require or support account creation. No login credentials are collected or stored.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
