Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a case?

Enter your USCIS receipt number (3 letters + 10 digits, e.g., EAC9999103403) on the Tracker tab and tap "Add Case." You can optionally add a nickname to help identify each case.

Are my cases saved when I close the app?

Currently, cases are stored in memory only and will be cleared when the app is closed. We recommend noting your receipt numbers separately. Persistent storage is planned for a future update.

Why am I seeing "Network request failed"?

This usually means there's a connectivity issue. Please check your internet connection and try again. The USCIS API may also occasionally be unavailable during maintenance windows.

What receipt number format is valid?

USCIS receipt numbers follow the format of 3 letters followed by 10 digits. Common prefixes include EAC, IOE, WAC, LIN, SRC, MSC, and YSC.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers. Case status requests go directly from your device to the official USCIS API. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What do the different tabs do?

Tracker - Add and monitor your USCIS cases. Bulletin - View the latest Visa Bulletin. Times - Check processing times by service center. News - Read USCIS news and alerts. Resources - Access helpful immigration links and tools.
